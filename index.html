Bio

Young software engineer and with the passion of startup product building using tech skills. We build chatbot/voicebots, blockchain and high end software solutions for the startups and brands.

I am a co-lead of Facebook Developers Circle, Ahmedabad and Co-Organizer at PyData Ahmedabad which is a local chapter of US based premier non-profit tech organization.
